Privacy Policy for The Modular Grid

v1.0.0

Effective Date: 18 May 2017

This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for the Chrome extension The Modular Grid (the “Extension”) and its website (the “Website”), located at http://the-modular-grid.org. This privacy notice applies solely to information collected by the Extension. It will notify you of the following:

  • What information we collect;
  • With whom it is shared; and
  • How policy changes will be communicated.

Information Collected

In order to obtain information about the Extension’s usage, we use Google Analytics to track statistics regarding home country, unique visitors, language, browser, and operating system. This data is collected and kept on Google’s servers and is not linked to any personal information that can identify any individual person.

In an effort to provide full transparency, the full source code, including Google Analytics code, for the Extension is available at https://github.com/code-warrior/the-modular-grid.

Sharing Information

The collected information is not shared with any third party, vendor, or individual. It is collected only to gauge insight into the Extension’s usage as a web development tool.

Notification of Changes

Whenever material changes are made to this privacy notice, the version number (currently v1.0.0) will be increased and links to previous versions of this privacy policy will be made available in this section.
